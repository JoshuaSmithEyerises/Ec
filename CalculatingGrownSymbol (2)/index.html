About

Our Purpose: Where our culture already affects the world of gaming in so many ways from music to storytelling, the Black Community has been a driving force in the global culture.

Our Goal: To increase the visibility and impact of Black Creatives in Gaming.

Our 3Cs:

  • Creativity - Creating games, developing space for Black graphic artists, and including black storytellers in gaming
  • Competition - Giving underprivileged BIPOC a competitive edge in the space
  • Culture - From Hip-Hop and Afrobeat to Voice acting to developing games
