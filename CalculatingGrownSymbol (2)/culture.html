Culture

"BLKHRZ is a company that is dedicated to supporting and uplifting Black individuals in the gaming industry. We strive to create a space where Black graphic artists, game developers, and storytellers can thrive and share their talents with the world. We work hard to develop games that showcase the unique perspectives and experiences of Black people, and we are committed to including Black voices in the creation and development of our projects. Through our efforts, we hope to foster a more inclusive and diverse gaming industry that reflects the experiences and stories of all its members. "
